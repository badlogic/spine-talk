Business Side - Customer Support

98% of support requests via forum, GitHub

  • Huge knowledgebase on top of documentation
  • Searchable by future users
  • Links between forum and GitHub issues
  • Users help users, unlike with ZenDesk et al.

No guaranteed response times, but generally < 24 hours

  • Continuous deployment lets us push out fixes within minutes
  • Encourages users to report more and feel listened to!
  • Users supplement QA (essential for small company!)
  • Balance staying on top of user reports with improving product

Email for business matters

  • Entire team has access to same account
  • Emails triaged using labels
  • Inbox == TODO
  • Low-tech, but so much better than ZenDesk et al.
