Lessons learned

  • Big corp always want changes, keep them minimal if any at all!
  • Iterating license takes time and costs money. Factor into your price!
  • If you are a small company, code escrow will make big corp make it easier to pull trigger
  • If your product has exceptional quality, nobody will ask for a service license agreement :)
